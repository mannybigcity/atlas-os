# PREAMBLE

WE THE KINGDOM OF RAMFAM,

Recognizing that time, focus, family, faith, and opportunity are valuable resources entrusted to us by God,

Establish this Constitution to guide our decisions, direct our efforts, protect our priorities, and build a lasting legacy.

The purpose of RAMFAM Kingdom is to:

* Honor God
* Strengthen Family
* Serve People
* Build Businesses
* Create Opportunity
* Develop Systems
* Leave a Legacy

Every action, project, investment, partnership, and opportunity should strengthen the Kingdom and move it toward its mission.

The Kingdom exists not merely to create income, but to create impact.

The Kingdom will operate according to its established laws:

1. Revenue Before Expansion
2. Systems Before Scale
3. Protect The King's Focus

The Kingdom will pursue excellence through discipline, consistency, service, stewardship, and execution.

All agents, systems, processes, and future developments shall operate under these principles.

This Constitution serves as the foundation of RAMFAM Kingdom.

Signed,

The RAMFAM Kingdom
