# HVAC LEAD GENERATION PLAYBOOK

Purpose: Build a repeatable system for finding HVAC companies that may need hats, shirts, polos, patches, signs, decals, banners, or website/business support.

## Recommended Agent Team

### MASON — System Builder

Role: Designs the lead generation system.

Mason should:

* Create the workflow
* Build the tracking process
* Define the steps from prospect discovery to follow-up
* Make the system repeatable

### SCOUT — Intelligence Director

Role: Finds HVAC companies and researches opportunities.

Scout should:

* Search for HVAC companies in Cypress, Tomball, Waller, Jersey Village, Spring, and NW Houston
* Identify companies with weak branding, outdated websites, or active hiring
* Look for companies using uniforms, trucks, hats, patches, decals, or yard signs
* Gather public business information

### HUNTER — Revenue Commander

Role: Qualifies and prioritizes prospects.

Hunter should:

* Score each lead by revenue potential
* Identify high-value targets
* Recommend which businesses to contact first
* Focus on companies likely to order hats, polos, tees, decals, or websites

### AMANDA — Outreach Director

Role: Handles approved communication.

Amanda should:

* Prepare outreach messages
* Follow up with prospects after Manny approval
* Track responses
* Help move interested prospects toward quotes

### DAVID — CRM Manager

Role: Stores and tracks all lead data.

David should:

* Keep prospect records organized
* Track contact status
* Track follow-up dates
* Track quote status
* Keep the pipeline clean

### ATLAS — Chief of Staff

Role: Coordinates the entire mission.

Atlas should:

* Assign agent responsibilities
* Review progress
* Recommend the next action
* Protect Manny’s time
* Keep the system aligned with Revenue First

## Standard Workflow

1. Scout finds HVAC companies.
2. Hunter scores and prioritizes them.
3. David stores them in CRM.
4. Mason improves the system.
5. Amanda drafts outreach.
6. Manny approves before anyone is contacted.
7. Atlas reviews progress and recommends next steps.

## Kingdom Rules

* Revenue first.
* Manny approves outreach before sending.
* Start local before expanding.
* Track every prospect.
* Follow up professionally.
* Build once, reuse often.
