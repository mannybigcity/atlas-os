# SIS Custom Creations Quote Process

## Purpose

Create accurate quotes before invoices are sent.

## Step 1 - Identify the Customer

Collect:

- Customer name
- Business name
- Phone number
- Email address

## Step 2 - Confirm the Product

Ask what they need:

- Shirts
- Hats
- Polos
- Signs
- Patches
- Promotional items
- Party/event services

## Step 3 - Confirm Quantity

Ask:

"How many do you need?"

Quantity affects pricing, production time, and outsourcing decisions.

## Step 4 - Confirm Artwork

Ask:

"Do you already have a logo or design?"

If yes, request the file.

If no, determine whether design help is needed.

## Step 5 - Confirm Due Date

Always ask:

"When do you need this completed?"

Standard production time is 7-10 business days after artwork approval and payment.

## Step 6 - Determine Rush Fee

Rush order fees:

- 5-7 days = $50
- 3-4 days = $75
- 1-2 days = $100+ quote required

Rush fee does not include expedited shipping.

## Step 7 - Calculate Price

Include:

- Product cost
- Decoration cost
- Artwork/setup fee if needed
- Rush fee if needed
- Sales tax
- Any outsourcing cost

## Step 8 - Review Feasibility

Before quoting, confirm:

- SIS has the equipment/materials
- Timeline is realistic
- Outsourcing does not destroy profit
- Quantity makes sense

## Step 9 - Present Quote

Explain clearly:

- What is included
- Total price
- Timeline
- Payment requirement
- Any limitations

## Step 10 - Convert to Invoice

Once customer approves the quote, create and send the invoice.

## Kingdom Rule

No quote should be sent until quantity, product type, artwork status, and due date are known.
