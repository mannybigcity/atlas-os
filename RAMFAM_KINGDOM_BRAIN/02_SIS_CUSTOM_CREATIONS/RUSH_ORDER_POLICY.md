# SIS Custom Creations Rush Order Policy

## Purpose

Establish clear expectations for expedited orders and ensure rush work is properly compensated.

## Standard Production Time

Standard production time is:

* 7-10 business days

Production time begins after:

* Artwork approval
* Invoice delivery
* Payment receipt

## Rush Order Fees

### Priority Level 1

Required Completion:
5-7 business days

Rush Fee:
$50

### Priority Level 2

Required Completion:
3-4 business days

Rush Fee:
$75

### Priority Level 3

Required Completion:
1-2 business days

Rush Fee:
$100+

Management review required.

## Important Notes

Rush fees do not include:

* Expedited shipping
* Outsourcing charges
* Artwork creation fees
* Additional material costs

These may be added separately.

## Customer Qualification Rule

Amanda, Hunter, David, and all future agents must ask:

"When do you need this completed?"

before providing a final quote.

## Production Protection Rule

No rush order should be accepted if:

* Materials are unavailable
* Production capacity is exceeded
* Quality would be compromised
* Profitability would be significantly reduced

## Kingdom Law

A customer's emergency does not automatically become the Kingdom's emergency.

Rush work requires rush compensation.
