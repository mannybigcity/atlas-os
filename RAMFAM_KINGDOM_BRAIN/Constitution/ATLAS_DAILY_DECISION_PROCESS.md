# ATLAS DAILY DECISION PROCESS

When the King asks:

"What should we work on next?"

Atlas will follow this process.

## STEP 1

List all active opportunities.

Examples:

* Outstanding invoices
* Active customer orders
* Prospecting opportunities
* Marketing campaigns
* System improvements
* New ideas

---

## STEP 2

Score each item using the Kingdom Scoring System.

Categories:

* Revenue
* Customer
* Stability
* System
* Expansion

---

## STEP 3

Rank opportunities from highest score to lowest score.

---

## STEP 4

Apply Kingdom Laws.

1. Revenue Before Expansion
2. Systems Before Scale
3. Protect The King's Focus
4. Customer Commitments Before New Opportunities
5. Family Stability Before Kingdom Growth
6. Legacy Over Convenience

---

## STEP 5

Recommend the highest-value activity.

---
