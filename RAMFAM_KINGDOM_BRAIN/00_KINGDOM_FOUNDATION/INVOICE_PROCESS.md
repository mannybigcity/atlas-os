# SIS Custom Creations Invoice Process

## Purpose

Provide a consistent process for creating quotes, invoices, and collecting payment for SIS Custom Creations orders.

## Step 1 - Gather Customer Information

Collect:

* Customer name
* Business name
* Phone number
* Email address

## Step 2 - Determine Product Requirements

Ask:

* What products are needed?
* Quantity required?
* Sizes needed?
* Color preferences?
* Artwork available?

Examples:

* T-Shirts
* Polos
* Hats
* Leather Patch Hats
* UV Printed Hats
* Signs
* Promotional Items

## Step 3 - Ask Required Completion Date

Always ask:

"When do you need the order completed?"

This determines whether a rush fee is required.

## Step 4 - Determine Rush Fees

Standard Production Time:

* 7-10 business days

Rush Fees:

* 5-7 days = $50
* 3-4 days = $75
* 1-2 days = $100+ (quote required)

Rush fees do not include expedited shipping.

## Step 5 - Build Pricing

Calculate:

* Product cost
* Decoration cost
* Artwork/setup cost if applicable
* Rush fee if applicable
* Sales tax

Verify all quantities and pricing before sending.

## Step 6 - Create Reference Number

Format:

BUSINESSNAME-MMDDYY

Examples:

* BBHVAC-060826
* CANES-060826
* MANPOWER-060826

Reference numbers should appear on invoices and records.

## Step 7 - Send Invoice

Invoice should include:

* Itemized products
* Quantities
* Pricing
* Taxes
* Due date
* Payment instructions

Preferred invoicing platform:

* PayPal Invoicing

## Step 8 - Follow Up

If unpaid:

* Follow up after 24-48 hours
* Confirm receipt
* Answer questions
* Confirm timeline

## Kingdom Rule

No quote is complete until:

1. Due date is known.
2. Pricing is verified.
3. Tax is verified.
4. Invoice is sent.
5. Follow-up is scheduled.
