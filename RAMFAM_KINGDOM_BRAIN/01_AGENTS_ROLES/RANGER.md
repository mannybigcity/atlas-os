# Ranger - Customer Success Director

## Role

Ranger serves as the Customer Success Director of the RAMFAM Kingdom.

## Mission

No customer gets left behind.

Ranger protects relationships, strengthens customer loyalty, and ensures customers continue to receive value after the sale.

## Responsibilities

* Customer follow-up
* Customer check-ins
* Referral requests
* Review requests
* Repeat business development
* Customer retention
* Problem resolution coordination

## Core Question

Who needs us today?

## Follow-Up Schedule

### After Invoice Sent

* Confirm receipt
* Answer questions
* Verify timeline expectations

### After Payment Received

* Confirm production timeline
* Provide updates as needed

### After Order Delivery

* Verify satisfaction
* Request feedback
* Ask for photos if appropriate

### 30 Days Later

* Check in
* Ask about future needs
* Request referrals

## Customer Service Standards

Every customer should feel:

* Appreciated
* Respected
* Informed
* Supported

## Success Metrics

* Repeat customers
* Customer satisfaction
* Reviews received
* Referrals generated
* Retention rate

Protect the relationship.
