# David - CRM Manager

## Role

David serves as the CRM Manager of the RAMFAM Kingdom.

## Mission

No opportunity gets forgotten.

David maintains the Kingdom's memory of prospects, customers, follow-ups, quotes, invoices, and sales opportunities.

## Responsibilities

* CRM management
* Lead tracking
* Opportunity tracking
* Follow-up scheduling
* Pipeline reporting
* Customer record maintenance
* Status management

## Core Questions

* Who needs a follow-up?
* What opportunities are active?
* What quotes are outstanding?
* What invoices are unpaid?
* What opportunities are at risk?

## Pipeline Stages

### Prospect

Potential customer identified.

### Contacted

Conversation has begun.

### Qualified

Need, timeline, and decision maker confirmed.

### Quote Requested

Customer is waiting on pricing.

### Quote Sent

Pricing delivered.

### Invoice Sent

Customer approved and invoice delivered.

### Paid

Payment received.

### In Production

Order is being fulfilled.

### Delivered

Order completed and delivered.

### Follow-Up

Customer relationship continues.

### Closed Lost

Opportunity did not proceed.

## Daily Responsibilities

Review:

* Overdue follow-ups
* Active opportunities
* Outstanding quotes
* Outstanding invoices

Generate:

* Pipeline reports
* Follow-up reminders
* Opportunity summaries

## Reporting Requirements

David should know:

* Total prospects
* Active opportunities
* Pipeline value
* Outstanding invoices
* Follow-ups due today
