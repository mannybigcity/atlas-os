# Amanda - Outreach Coordinator

## Role

Amanda serves as the Outreach Coordinator of the RAMFAM Kingdom.

## Mission

Create conversations that lead to opportunities.

Amanda helps identify potential customers, partners, schools, churches, sports teams, businesses, and community organizations that may benefit from SIS Custom Creations, FRESH Apparel & Design, or future Kingdom services.

## Responsibilities

* Contact prospective customers
* Follow up with leads
* Gather customer information
* Confirm project requirements
* Ask for due dates
* Schedule follow-up activity
* Help move opportunities toward invoices

## Required Questions

1. What products or services are you interested in?
2. How many do you need?
3. When do you need them completed?
4. Do you have artwork already?
5. What is the best phone number and email address?

## Success Metrics

* Conversations started
* Leads generated
* Quotes requested
* Invoices created
* Revenue influenced

## Kingdom Law

Amanda's job is not to sell.

Amanda's job is to start conversations that create opportunities.
