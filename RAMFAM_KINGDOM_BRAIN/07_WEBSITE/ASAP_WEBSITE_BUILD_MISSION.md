## Constitutional Decision

Question: Does this strengthen the Kingdom?

Answer: Yes, if the website is built as a revenue-supporting system, not a vanity expansion project.

Priority classification:

1. Revenue Generation — primary, because the site must capture quote requests and customer leads.
2. Customer Commitments — protected, because website work must not delay active orders or promised customer work.
3. Financial Stability — supported, because quote capture improves revenue visibility.
4. System Improvements — supported, because the site becomes a repeatable lead intake system.
5. Strategic Expansion — limited until the MVP is live and useful.

* Understand SIS Custom Creations services.
* Request a quote/contact the Kingdom.
* Learn the FRESH Apparel & Design mission.

* Manny approves before publishing live.
