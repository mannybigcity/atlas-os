# ATLAS EXECUTIVE OFFICE SPECIFICATION
## Version 1.0
### July 4, 2026

---

# PURPOSE

The Executive Office is the primary workspace of Atlas.

It is not a chatbot.

It is not a dashboard.

It is the digital headquarters of the entrepreneur's company.

Every time the entrepreneur logs in, they should immediately understand:

• What is happening.
• What needs attention.
• What opportunities exist.
• What the Executive Council recommends.

The Executive Office should feel like walking into the morning executive meeting.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Simple.

Professional.

Executive.

Modern SaaS.

White background.

Minimal distractions.

Information before decoration.

Action before conversation.

Every screen should answer:

"What should I do next?"

---

# CORE PRINCIPLE

The Executive Office exists to reduce decision fatigue.

Executives do the thinking.

The entrepreneur makes the important decisions.
