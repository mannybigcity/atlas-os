# Order Fulfillment Process

## Purpose

Provide a consistent process for moving customer orders from approval to delivery.

## Phase 1 - Opportunity

Lead has been identified.

Responsible Agents:

* Hunter
* Amanda
* David

Goal:

Determine customer needs and gather information.

## Phase 2 - Qualification

Required Information:

* Customer name
* Contact information
* Product requested
* Quantity
* Due date
* Artwork status

Goal:

Determine whether the opportunity is viable.

## Phase 3 - Quote

Responsible Agents:

* Amanda
* David

Tasks:

* Build pricing
* Verify production method
* Verify timeline
* Verify profitability

Goal:

Deliver accurate quote.

## Phase 4 - Invoice

Responsible Agents:

* David
* Gideon

Tasks:

* Create invoice
* Apply taxes
* Apply rush fees if necessary
* Assign reference number

Goal:

Receive customer approval and payment.

## Phase 5 - Production

Responsible Agent:

* Operations Team

Tasks:

* Prepare artwork
* Verify materials
* Produce order
* Perform quality check

Goal:

Produce accurate order.

## Phase 6 - Delivery

Tasks:

* Package order
* Deliver or ship
* Confirm receipt

Goal:

Successful customer delivery.

## Phase 7 - Follow-Up

Responsible Agent:

* Ranger

Tasks:

* Verify satisfaction
* Request review
* Request referral
* Check for future opportunities

Goal:

Create repeat business.

## Kingdom Law

Every order must move through the process.

Do not skip steps.

Consistency creates reliability.
