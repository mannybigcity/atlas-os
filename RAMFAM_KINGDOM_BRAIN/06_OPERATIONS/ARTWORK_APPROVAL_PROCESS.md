# Artwork Approval Process

## Purpose

Protect the customer and the Kingdom by ensuring artwork is reviewed and approved before production begins.

## Kingdom Rule

No production begins until artwork is approved.

Verbal approval is not sufficient.

Written approval is preferred.

## Step 1 - Receive Artwork

Obtain:

* Logo files
* Artwork files
* Text requirements
* Color requirements
* Placement instructions

Preferred file formats:

* AI
* EPS
* SVG
* PDF
* PNG (high resolution)

## Step 2 - Review Artwork

Verify:

* Correct spelling
* Correct phone numbers
* Correct website addresses
* Correct colors
* Correct logo version
* Correct sizing

## Step 3 - Create Mockup

Prepare a visual proof showing:

* Product color
* Artwork placement
* Artwork size
* Front design
* Back design
* Sleeve design if applicable

## Step 4 - Send Proof

Send proof to customer.

Request approval before production.

Recommended wording:

"Please review carefully. Production will begin after approval is received."

## Step 5 - Customer Approval

Acceptable approvals:

* Email approval
* Text message approval
* Written approval
* Signed proof

Save approval records whenever possible.

## Step 6 - Revision Requests

If revisions are requested:

* Update artwork
* Create revised proof
* Resubmit for approval

Repeat until approved.

## Step 7 - Final Approval

Once approval is received:

* Mark artwork approved
* Save approved proof
* Move order into production

## Common Issues To Check

### Spelling

Verify all names and text.

### Dates

Verify years and event dates.

### Contact Information

Verify phone numbers and websites.

### Colors

Verify customer expectations.

### Placement

Verify location and size.

## Kingdom Law

If it is not approved, it is not ready for production.

Approval protects the customer and protects the Kingdom.
