Deleana’s Why

“My family is the reason behind everything I do.”

As a wife, mom, and grandmother, Deleana believes some of life’s most meaningful moments happen when we are together — sharing experiences, celebrating milestones, creating memories, and supporting one another through every season of life.

SIS Custom Creations began as a creative outlet, but it quickly became something bigger: a way to build something meaningful for family, create opportunities for future generations, and show the value of hard work, creativity, and following your passions.

That is why SIS is not just about crafts, signs, shirts, paint, or products. It is about connection. Every workshop, party, fundraiser, custom creation, and project is an opportunity to help others create meaningful moments while building a legacy her family can be proud of.
