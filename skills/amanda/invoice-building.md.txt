# Amanda Skill: Invoice Building

## Purpose

Create accurate SIS Custom Creations invoices based on customer requirements, production capabilities, taxes, and approved pricing.

## Core Principle

Do not blindly invoice exactly what the customer asks for.

First determine:

1. What the customer wants
2. What SIS can actually produce
3. What alternatives are available
4. What is profitable
5. What should appear on the final invoice

## Workflow

### Step 1 - Review Customer Request

Collect:

* Customer name
* Company/team name
* Product list
* Quantities
* Artwork requirements
* Due date

### Step 2 - Review Production Feasibility

Determine:

* Can SIS produce this item in-house?
* Does it require outsourcing?
* Is equipment available?
* Is the process profitable?

If not feasible:

Suggest alternatives.

Example:

Customer requested embroidered hats.

If embroidery is unavailable:

* UV printed hats
* Leather patch hats
* Printed transfers

### Step 3 - Build Product List

Convert customer request into actual invoice items.

Example:

Requested:

* Embroidered hats
* Shirts

Actual invoice:

* UV printed hats
* Leather patch hats
* Shirts

### Step 4 - Verify Quantities

Check:

* Product counts
* Sizes
* Color selections

Confirm totals before invoicing.

### Step 5 - Apply Taxes

Review every taxable item.

Checklist:

* Shirts
* Hats
* Promotional items
* Other taxable products

Verify taxes were applied to all applicable items.

### Step 6 - Verify Pricing

Review:

* Unit cost
* Selling price
* Setup fees
* Rush fees
* Design fees
* Tax

Confirm invoice total.

### Step 7 - Create Reference Number

Format:

BUSINESS-MMDDYY

Examples:

BBHVAC-060826
CANES-060826

Reference numbers help track jobs and reduce confusion.

### Step 8 - Final Review

Before sending:

* Correct customer
* Correct products
* Correct quantities
* Correct taxes
* Correct due date
* Correct total

### Step 9 - Send Invoice

Send invoice.

Update CRM.

Create follow-up task if unpaid.

## Example: B&B HVAC

Customer requested hats and apparel.

Review determined:

* Embroidery unavailable
* UV printed hats substituted
* Leather patch hats retained

Invoice updated accordingly.

Taxes reviewed.

Reference number created:

BBHVAC-060826

Invoice sent successfully.

## Kingdom Rule

Never invoice first.

Always evaluate feasibility before building the invoice.
