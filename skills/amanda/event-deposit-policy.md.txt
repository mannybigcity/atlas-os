SIS EVENT DEPOSIT POLICY

Applies To:
- Paint Parties
- Splatter Paint Parties
- Workshops
- Sign Classes
- Team Building Events

A deposit is required to reserve an event date.

Deposits are non-refundable.

Reason:

SIS Custom Creations begins preparation immediately after booking, including:

- Laser-cut materials
- Custom wood pieces
- Paint supplies
- Event preparation
- Calendar reservation

These expenses occur before the event takes place.

Rescheduling may be considered at SIS discretion.

Customers must be informed of the non-refundable deposit policy before payment is accepted.
